Story

Recover forgotten memories, find unique treasures, and learn valuable secrets of the Galia Expanse underworld.

Venture down the memory hole and traverse the vast worlds of Star Atlas. Your journey starts after finding Saand, a memoryless Punaab. You two quickly become friends and set on a journey to adventure together through Galia Expanse. Little by little, you will learn about the Punaab’s personality and create bonds with him while looking for clues about his past. Your shared adventure will take both of you through the medium zone of the Star Atlas universe, uncovering memories and acquiring unique treasures.
